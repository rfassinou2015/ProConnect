Hiring on Outsource.com

Save your business time & money by outsourcing your work. Outsource.com connects you with top-quality freelancers that are available to work on your projects.

« What is Outsource.com?

Outsource.com is a secure, online marketplace where individuals and businesses can safely and easily outsource their projects, while saving time and money. As a client on Outsource.com, you will be able to post jobs, evaluate freelancer quotes, and find solutions for your business or individual needs.

« How does Outsource.com work?

Outsource.com connects you with experienced freelancers that will provide you with business solutions. All you need to do is sign-up, post a job, and you will begin receiving quotes from our talented freelancers. From here, you will evaluate quotes, choose the candidate that is the best fit for your project, as well as set up a payment plan and contract with the freelancer.

« Why should I use Outsource.com?

Outsource.com is a smarter and easier way to outsource your projects and find a freelancer that fits your needs. We will help save you time and money by allowing you to compare, negotiate with, and pay experienced freelancers. Our goal is to provide you with the highest quality freelancers so your project will be completed successfully and on time.

« What are the benefits of Outsource.com?

As a client on Outsource.com, you will have access to a variety of innovative tools to keep the hiring process simple and easy.

Finding Experts
  • We pride ourselves in offering the most qualified freelancers so you can always find a solution for your business needs.
Recommended Freelancers
  • To ease your search, we match you with qualified freelancers who obtain the experience you desire.
Feedback / Reviews
  • Read reviews from real people and get an idea of your freelancer's work ethic before the job begins.
Customer Support
  • Our customer support team is here when you need it most. We will help you in times of difficulty and get you back on the right path.

« How do I select the best freelancers?
Evaluating a Freelancer

Once you have began receiving quotes, it is time to carefully analyze them and select a few candidates that fit the project requirements. The candidates will have different strengths, skillsets, and reviews from previous clients may also vary. This could make the hiring process difficult, so here are a few steps to take to ensure you are selecting the best candidate for the project.

1) Carefully Read the Quote

A well-written quote should be personalized to fit your specific needs, demonstrates strong knowledge of the subject, suggests a firm understanding of the project's goals, provides a solid outline for their work schedule, and their outline falls within the time frame as well as your overall budget.

2) Review their Profile

Once you have carefully read the candidate's proposal and made sure they fit your needs, it is now time to review the candidate's profile. Here's what to look for:

  • Overview: The overview section should provide a brief background of the freelancer, describe what exactly it is that they do, describe some of the successful projects they have completed, explain the problems in which they can solve, and illustrates the freelancer's ability to effectively communicate their achievements.
  • Portfolio: The portfolio allows you to see the freelancer's work firsthand. You can browse through their previous projects to see the quality of their work and if any of it is applicable to your project.
  • Skills: Here, you will be able to view all the freelancer's skills and see if they match up with the job requirements.
  • Reviews: The review section allows you to read real comments from previous employers and get an idea of the freelancer's ability to successfully complete projects.
  • Experience: The experience section allows you to view the jobs the freelancer previously held, certifications they have acquired, and their education.

After you have examined the freelancer's profile and feel as if they are still a great candidate for the job, it is time to set up an interview.

3) Contact the freelancers about the job

It is important to contact the freelancer if you are interested in hiring them. We encourage you to follow-up with a few freelancers before deciding who to hire.

  • Ask a few questions
    Ask each freelancer to answer a few questions for you so that you can get a better understanding of what they can offer you.
  • Setup a call or Skype meeting
    If necessary, you can setup a time to discuss the project directly with the freelancer. We especially recommend doing this for larger, more complicated projects.
  • Hire the best freelancer for the job
    Once you have decided which freelancer you would like to hire, you can discuss with them directly about how to pay them for their work and set-up a schedule.
« What if I cannot find the freelancer I need?

As you may already know, it can be difficult to find the right applicant for a given job. Here are a few steps to help if you cannot find the freelancer you need on the first time:

1) Revise your job post.
  • Make sure your job post is detailed, the goals are clear, and you are accurately describing the project needing completion.
2) Contact us
  • Outsource.com is here for you along every step of the way. We have certified professionals that can help you get the results you desire.
« What if my freelancer is not working out?

If your project is not going as well as you planned, it may be time to rethink the outcome.

1) Communicate with your freelancer.
  • It can be difficult to strictly communicate via email. Sometimes a simple phone call can be the solution to an ongoing issue. Schedule a phone call or a video chat with your freelancer so you can reiterate the goals of the project and make sure you both are on the same page.
2) Follow your instincts.
  • As we mentioned before, you are the only person that knows exactly what is needed for the project to be successful. If those goals are not being met or if you don't feel the freelancer is giving this project their best effort, there is a large pool of freelancers that are willing to work hard and make sure the job will be completed.
3) File a dispute.
  • If you have done the best you can to communicate with your freelancer, and neither one of you is coming to a conclusion, you are able to file a dispute. To file a dispute, go to the Actions dropdown for the relevant job and click to "Dispute the Job".
« How do I write a quality job post?
Create a Job Post in 3 Steps
An attentive and well-developed job post is the best way to attract experienced freelancers. Clearly and concisely describing your job post will increase the quality of proposals you receive and allows freelancers to create custom proposals that fit your needs. Read below for more information on how to create a successful job post.
1) Job Title

Create a title that is eye-catching, definitive, and uses language which is vital for the position.

Bad
  • Looking for a developer

Don't do this! It's lifeless, undemanding, and is unspecific. The title sets the mood for the entire job post and you want to make sure you are not leading applicants away from the job post.

Good
  • Python / UNIX Developer for Growing Website
  • Social Media Guru / Content Writer for Food Blog

By creating a distinct job title that is directly related to the project, there will be an increase the quality of the job proposals received.

2) Job Description
Position

Draft a clear and concise description of the position, provide a list of required skills, set up a timeframe for the job, implement a maximum budget, establish work expectations, the type of communication you prefer, include questions for the applicant to answer, and make sure you convey the ultimate goal of the job. Jobs descriptions should be straight-forward, they should describe the applicant's responsibilities, and provide insight into the company so the applicant can fully understand the objective of the project.

You should begin receiving proposals within the first few days of the job post. Each job post is different, so you should be more concerned with the quality of the proposals you receive, rather than quantity. If you are not seeing the results you desire, please contact us at support@outsource.com and we will help get you on the right track.

3) Additional Details

Add anything to your post that might be beneficial or unique in attracting a certain type of freelancer. If you work in a niche or alternative market, feel free to ask for people who might have a background in what you need accomplished!

Try to format your description as professionally as possible and include as much as information as you can; well-written proposals will receive more and better quality proposals from our amazing and talented freelancers.

« How does the hiring process work?

Once you find a freelancer that you would like to hire, you are able to send an offer with the terms for the job. This offer will be sent to the freelancer who is then able to either accept or decline the offer, or ask for revisions. If the freelancer asks for revisions, you will be able to make updates to the offer and re-send it. Once the offer is accepted by the freelancer, they will be officially hired by you and will begin working.

« What happens when the freelancer accepts an offer?

Both parties have now agreed to the terms outlined and defined in the contract. Work can begin on the part of the freelancer and any deliverable has been funded or hourly pay periods can begin to take effect.

« How much should I pay a freelancer?

Pay rates for all freelance work can vary based on work experience, technical skills, specializations, as well as overall experience. So how do you know if the freelancer is charging you a fair rate?

1) Look at the freelancers portfolio.
  • How much experience does the freelancer have? Does the portfolio offer quality work? If so, the job may require a higher rate of pay.
2) How difficult is the job?
  • Do you need to hire someone that has a specialized skill? Is this a short term or long term position? How large is the workload? These factors, and many others, may cause the pay rate to fluctuate.
3) What is the market rate?
  • There are many websites that offer a pay scale for similar work.

« How can I pay my freelancers?

Outsource.com hosts a payment system wherein Clients can pay freelancers for the work that is completed on Outsource. How the payments are processed depends on whether the job is setup as fixed-price or hourly. For fixed-price jobs, the client is able to divide the project into separate deliverables and deposit funds for each deliverable. The payments will be held by Outsource.com until the work is completed and the Client releases the payments. For hourly work, the freelancer will enter the hours that they work each week and the Client will be charged on Sunday at Midnight UTC for work completed by the freelancer in the previous week.

« How do I enter and update my payment information?

Outsource.com accepts all major credit and debit cards as well as PayPal to pay for work provided by freelancers. You will be asked to enter a payment method the first time that you send an offer to a client. You can update your payment method at anytime in the Account Information section.

« What is a deliverable?

For fixed-price jobs, the work can be divided into deliverables. A deliverable is a smaller or incremental part of a larger job that a client and a freelancer have agreed to on Outsource.com. Deliverables are used to gauge the progress of jobs and allow for payment to be made in smaller amounts rather than a lump sum at the completion of a job.

« How do I fund the first deliverable?

Once your offer is sent to the freelancer of your choosing, the first deliverable will automatically be funded should they choose to accept. This is why payment information is necessary before the quote is sent to the freelancer.

« How do I monitor the status of my projects?

It is your responsibility to communicate with the freelancer in order to ensure the work is being completed to your satisfaction. For hourly jobs, the freelancer will enter a short description whenever adding hours worked.

« How do I close a job once it has been completed?

Once the last scheduled payment has been completed, you can close the job in the project page. Any outstanding funds that are owed on a job will still be paid to the freelancer. This will prevent the freelancer from entering any more hours worked for the job and allow you to leave a review for the freelancer.

« What if I have a dispute?

Should a client become unsatisfied with work completed by a freelancer, they are allowed to open a dispute against the freelancer on Outsource.com

Prior to filing a dispute, Outsource.com strongly recommends trying to resolve the issue directly with the freelancer. To file a dispute, go to the Actions dropdown for the relevant job and click to "Dispute the Job".

« Why do I need to include my location?

As a client, your address, city, and state are optional fields. However you must include your timezone as well as the country in which you reside. Outsource.com requires you enter your location for safety purposes.

« How do I change my password?

Changing your password is simple and hassle-free. Once logged into your account, go into your Account Info . Choose the Password Settings tab and a link with instructions will be sent to your email.

If you are locked out of your account, please go to the login page and click the link that says Reset Password. From here, we will send you an email with a link to reset your password.

« How do I change my email preferences?

If you are unsatisfied with the emails you receive, you can easily customize your email preferences. To do so, go into your Account Info, choose the Email Notification tab, and you will be able to customize the emails you receive.

« How do I delete my account?

If you are having any issues with Outsource.com or you would like to deactive your account, please email us at support@outsource.com with the emaill adress that is associated with your Outsource.com account or contact us by phone at (866) 784-1265.

« Why are reviews important?

Leaving reviews for previous freelancers is important because it helps build their reputation and future clients will be able to see all the reviews left by previous clients. The review section is a great spot to share your experience with the freelancer and whether or not you would recommend the freelancer to other clients.

« Is any of my personal information shown on my profile?

We take your privacy seriously, so we do not display any of your personal information publicly. In order to see what information is displayed, click the View Job button displayed in your My Jobs folder and you can view exactly what the freelancer sees. If you would like to change the information that is displayed, you can update your account settings or contact us at support@outsource.com.

« Who can see my profile?

Other clients and freelancers will be unable to view your account. Once a job has been posted, a freelancer will only be able to see your first name along with your last initial. Also, the freelancer will only be allowed to contact you through Outsource.com.

Admin & Customer Support Management, Finance, & Legal Sales & Marketing

Outsource.com also gives you full access to your account settings so you can choose the information that freelancers will see.

You can have peace of mind knowing Outsource.com does not sell or share your personal information with others. Please see our Privacy Policy for more information about which information is shared and which will never be shared with anyone.
