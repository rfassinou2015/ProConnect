Freelancing on Outsource.com

Complete your profile and Outsource.com will connect you with high quality jobs so you can begin building your freelance career, today.

« What is Outsource.com?

Outsource.com is a secure, online marketplace where individuals and businesses can safely and easily outsource their jobs to a large talent pool of freelancers worldwide. As a freelancer on Outsource.com, we will provide you with the resources necessary to showcase your talents, allowing you to connect with thousands of business owners in need of your services.

« How does Outsource.com work?

Outsource.com will connect you with clients who are in need your services. All you need to do is sign up, build a detailed profile, and you will be connected to businesses and individuals who need your help. Outsource.com is a subscription based website where you are able to advertise your freelance services to clients.

« Why should I use Outsource.com?

Outsource.com is a smarter and easier way to grow your business and connect with clients looking to hire someone with your skillset. We will match you with recommended projects, connect you with clients, and help you every step of the way so you can get the best results. We also balance the quote to job post ratio, so that your chances of being hired are very high.

« What are the benefits of Outsource.com?

As a freelancer on Outsource.com, you will have access to a variety of innovative tools to keep the job search simple and easy.

Finding Businesses and Individuals
  • Connect with future clients that are ready to hire you
Specific Job Requests
  • To ease your search, we will only match you with clients that need to hire your specific skillset
Customer Support
  • Our customer support team is here when you need it most. We will help you in times of difficulty and get you back on the right path
« How do I get started?

Getting started is quick and simple.

1) Sign up
  • If you haven't already, sign up as a freelancer and begin receive job requests today
2) Build Your Profile.
  • Make it a point to articulate your skills, highlight your core competencies, upload your professional portfolio, include any relevant certifications or test scores to help boost your credibility, and upload a professional photo of yourself. The more information you provide on your profile page, the better chance you have of getting hired.
3) Quote Jobs
  • You can browse jobs manually and we will send you regular job requests (you can change your email settings in the account info section of your profile).
4) Choose Your Membership Package
  • We have three different 6 month subscription packages for you to choose from. Each package gives you a monthly allotment of credits to use towards sending quotes.
« Is Outsource.com available on mobile?

Outsource.com does not have a mobile application, however our website is mobile user friendly.

« How do I contact customer service?

We have a great team here at Outsource.com and we would love to hear from you at support@outsource.com or you can call us at (866) 784-1265.  Our support team works hard in order to get you an answer in a timely manner.  Please allow up to one business day for a response.

« How do I quote a job?

Applying for a job is simple and easy. There are a few ways in which a freelancer can apply for a project:

1) New Job Request Emails
  • We will send you an email when there is a job posted that matches the services you provide.
2) Job Requests tab
  • This gives you a list of all projects that may match your skills.
3) Choose Your Membership
  • If you do not have a membership, you will be prompted to choose from one of our packages at the time of sending a quote.
« What are credits?

In order to maintain a high quality marketplace for both freelancers and clients, we require freelancers to purchase credits before sending quotes to clients. The number of credits required to send a quote is dependent on the size of the budget selected by the client. By clicking on the job description, you are able to see how many credits are required for each job.

« How many credits are needed for a quote?

The number of credits required to send a quote varies depending on the size of the budget set for the job. It could cost between 3-9 credits to send a quote. If your quote is not read by the client within 5 days of sending, then we will refund the credits used for that quote back to your account.

« Why do I have to purchase credits before quoting a job?

The 6 month subscriptions allow us to heavily discount the cost to send the quotes.   You are saving a minimum of 49% with our packages.   We realize it is a large commitment upfront, but we will able to provide you with enough opportunities to find work through our website to make it worth the investment.   In addition, we include a Guarantee that if you are not hired by a client during the 6 month membership, then we will give you an additional 6 months of the same membership absolutely free.  This is our commitment to providing a great marketplace.

« How do I find jobs that fit my skills?

As a freelancer, you will be able to browse through jobs. Jobs are matched accordingly to the categories you sign up with. These categories can be updated in the "Skills" tab in the "My Profile" section of your account. Outsource.com allows for optional email updates on jobs that fit your skills. It is important to build a strong profile that emphasizes all your skills, displays your pay rate, as well as specifies the categories and subcategories in which you specialize. Including as much specific information as possible will allow you to find jobs that best fit your expertise.

« How much should I charge for my work?

Pay rates for all freelance work can vary based on work experience, technical skills, specializations, as well as overall experience. So how do you know if the client is asking for a fair rate?

1) How difficult is the job?
  • Does the job require a specialized skill or certification? Is this a short term or long term position? How large is the workload? These factors, and many others, may cause the pay rate to fluctuate.
2) What is the market rate?
  • There are many websites that provide the going rate for a specific type of work.
« Is this full-time or part-time work?

Each job will specify whether the work is for a one time project, part-time, or full time work.

« What do subscriptions cost?

Credits are assigned in accordance with the monthly subscription that you have selected with Outsource.com.   Before sending your first quote, you must select a subscription in order to begin quoting clients.  We offer multiple types of subscriptions, either three or six month memberships that are billed as one time payments:

  • Value 6 months: 29.99 per month for a 40 credits per month subscription, total $179.94 (Save 49%).
  • Value 3 months: 34.99 per month for a 40 credits per month subscription, total $104.97 (Save 54%).
  • Standard 6 months: 19.99 per month for a 20 credits per month subscription, total $119.94.
  • Standard 3 months: 22.99 per month for a 30 credits per month subscription, total $68.97.

All of the subscriptions are billed upfront for the 6 months and will renew at the same price.  All of the 6 month subscriptions also include the Outsource.com Guarantee which guarantees that if you do not find work during the 6 month subscription, you will receive another 6 months free.

« How do I purchase credits?

You can purchase credits in 4 easy steps:

  • Find a job you would like to apply for (whether that be via email or manual search).
  • Begin drafting your quote, including background information about yourself, the services you provide, as well as a price.
  • Click Submit Your Quote and a credit popup will appear.
  • Click Continue and you will be able to purchase credits as well as submit your quote to the client.
« What services can I offer?

Outsource.com offers a large pool of clients that are in need of your expertise. Client's projects include, but are not limited to:

  • Administrative & Customer Support
  • Management, Finance, & Legal
  • Sales and Marketing
  • Software & IT
  • Web, Design, & Media
  • Writing & Translation
« What should I include in my quote?

In the message, you should include the following:

Brief Introduction

You should include a brief introduction of yourself which includes your skills, expertise, and how you will be able to solve the client's problem.

Description

Be sure to include a detailed description of how you will complete the client's job, what methods you will use, and make sure you answer any of the client's questions.

Rate of Pay
Include your hourly rate for the job, or set up a deliverable system so you can get paid as you accomplish tasks.
Differentiate

Differentiate yourself from the other candidates and make sure you tell the client why you will be the best freelancer for the job.

« Can I see which jobs I have quoted previously?

Yes, once you are logged into your account you will be able to see the jobs you have quoted previously by clicking the Sent Quotes tab on the top of the page. You will be able to see all the quotes that have been sent recently as well as archived quotes.

« Can I edit / update a quote after I have already sent it?

You are able to update the price estimate in a quote once it has been submitted. This can be done by clicking Sent Quotes on the top of the page and clicking on the quote. There is no way to edit the original message that you sent along with the original price quote, but you can send follow-up messages which do not cost any additional credits.

« What happens after I send the quote?

Once the quote has been sent, it is up to the client to get in contact with you in order to further discuss the job details.   You can also send a follow-up message, which will not cost you additional credits, if you have any questions.

« What happens if the client doesn't view my quote?

Outsource.com understands that there may be times when you will not get the job. In the case that a client does not open your quote after 5 days, the credits will be returned to your account for use towards another quote.

« Can I buy more credits if I run out?

Absolutely. If you run out of the monthly allotted amount of credits you may purchase more at the time that you wish to send your next quote.  After the monthly credits run out, you can purchase additional credits per credit or in a bundle.  The pricing for additional credits is:

  • 1 Credit ($1.67)
  • 24 Credits ($1.46/each) (save 12%)
  • 60 Credits ($1.42/each) (save 15%)
« When are credits returned?

Outsource.com will return any credits used to send a quote in the event that a client does not view your quote within 5 days of sending. The credits will be returned to your account for use towards another job and you will also be notified of this transaction via email.

« Do my credits expire?

Credits do not expire. Any unused credits will roll over to the next month.

« Do I have to use my credits for every quote?

Yes, you have to use credits for every quote that you send. However, you do not have to send a quote for every job request.

« How do I get paid on Outsource.com?

In order to collect funds for services provided and jobs completed on Outsource.com as a freelancer, you will be able to receive funds through ACH (U.S. only), PayPal, Wire Transer, eCheck (certain countries only), and a mailed check. To withdraw a payment, go to "Receive Payments" in the account section.

« Where does my account information go and what is needed?

Outsource.com uses Tipalti Inc. to securely send payments to freelancers providing services on Outsource.com. Specific account information that is needed in order to receive payment through Outsource.com depends on the country of residence for the freelancer and will be asked when you setup your withdrawal method for the first time.

« How does the payment service work?

The payment system varies on the payment determined by the client at the beginning of the contract as specified in the original negotiations. Clients that choose hourly contracts will be billed weekly for the hours worked by the freelancer on Sunday night for the previous week. Funds will be available to the freelancer on the following Thursday at Midnight UTC. Clients that choose fixed price contracts will set up deliverables that are to be funded on a per-diem basis throughout the length of the job. When the project is initially set up, the client automatically funds the first deliverable and the freelancer is free to begin working at their earliest convenience. Deliverables can be arranged by the freelancer and the client through the messaging service in the Job Project page.

« Where do I submit my work performed or the hours I worked on a job?

Freelancers are encouraged to upload their deliverables into the file section of the project page that both parties have access to. You can also enter the amount of hours worked for the week into this section, providing that it falls under the amount of hours limited that is specified in the contract.

« How do I get paid if I work hourly?

To get paid for hourly work completed, you must submit the number of hours that you complete work each week into the work log for each job. The clients will be charged for the number of hours that you entered the previous week on Sunday at midnight UTC. If there are no disputes filed by the client, then these funds will be made available on Thursday at midnight UTC. Should there be any Bank holidays, please allow for the specified extra time for the funds to be deposited.

« How do I get paid if I am working on a fixed contract?

Freelancers working on a paid contract will be paid upon the completion of deliverables that will be agreed on by both parties in the contract. The client will fund a payment for a deliverable when it is created. These funds will be held by Outsource.com until the client authorizes the release of payment. At that time, the funds will be made available to be withdrawn by the freelancer.

« What if I have a dispute? What if my work completed is in dispute?

Should issues arise between a client and freelancer, both parties are able to file a dispute with Outsource.com. Disputes will be settled in an appropriate and timely manner by the team at Outsource to help mediate any issues help by the two parties. The job that has been filed for dispute will be paused until the matter is resolved by Outsource.com.

« What if I am a non-American freelancer? Will I be taxed on my wages earned?

Non-US persons (as defined by the US Internal Revenue Service) are required to complete an online statement to that effect. Generally speaking, non-US persons are people or entities that have no United States activities including, but not limited to, owning a web server/hosting service in the United States, or having employees in the United States providing services that are involved in generating income. Disclaimer: Outsource does not provide tax advice. Payers and payees should consult their tax advisor to determine tax status and eligibility.

« How long do payments take to process?

Payments typically take 3-5 business days to process and be approved for final withdrawal.

« Why do I need to include my location?

As a freelancer, your address, city, state, country, and timezone are required. Outsource.com requires this information for safety purposes.

« Can I see a history of my credit purchases?

Yes, Outsource.com offers a transaction history for your credit purchases. Once logged in, your Credit History is located in the Account Info section.

« How do I change my password?

Changing your password is simple and hassle-free. Once logged into your account, go into your Account Info. Choose the Password Settings tab and a link with instructions will be sent to your email. If you are locked out of your account, please go to the login page and click the link that says Reset Password. From here, we will send you an email with a link to reset your password.

« How do I change my email preferences?

If you are unsatisfied with the emails you receive, you can easily customize your email preferences. To do so, go into your Account Info, choose the Email Notification tab, and you will be able to customize the emails you receive.

« How do I deactivate my account?

To deactivate your account, login to your account and click on the arrow next to your name in the top right corner. Then click on Account Info and then click on Deactivate Account to bring up the Deactivation page.

« Can I add reviews from previous clients?

To help build your reputation, Outsource.com allows you to request reviews from previous clients that were not found on Outsource.com. Simply go to your Profile, click the Reviews tab, and click Request Review From Previous Clients.

« Who can see my profile?

A client will only be able to see your profile if you send them a quote. No freelancers will be able to view your profile.

« Do I need to upload a photo?

While uploading a photo to your profile is optional, it is highly recommended. Adding a photo improves your chances of being hired, boosts your credibility with clients, and accounts for 15% of the profile completion rate.

« What can clients see when they view my profile?

Clients will be able to see any information that is included in the My Profile section. You can view your profile as a client in the My Profile section.

« Is any of my personal information shown on my profile?

We take your privacy seriously, so we make the information displayed on your profile customizable. In order to see what information is displayed on your profile, go to the Edit Profile section and click View Profile as a Client. Here, you will be able to see exactly what the client sees. If you would like to change the information that is displayed, you can edit your profile or contact us at support@outsource.com

« What do I add to my portfolio?

Your portfolio is an important part of your profile and you should include some of your most relevant work as well as recent work. Your portfolio is an important part of your profile because it shows clients what you have been working on as well as your capabilities as a freelancer. In order to build your online portfolio, you may upload JPG, PNG, GIF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, MP3, MP4, PPT and PPTX files under 4MB.

« What should I include in my profile description?

In your profile description, you should include the most relevant information about yourself as well as the services you are offering. This could include, but is not limited to:

  • A brief background of yourself
  • Descriptions of recent projects
  • Describing the problem you solve
  • How you can be of value to the client
« What do I include in the Experience section?

Your experience section should include any relevant certifications, your education, as well as your previous work history. This is where clients can verify your credentials and view your background.

« What skills can I add to my profile?

Relevant skills to include on your profile include Javascript, German, Accounting, etc. Outsource.com has a large list of potential skills you can add to your profile and if for any reason you do not find the skill you woud like to add, please email us at support@outsource.com so we can update our list.

Categories are the different job industries on Outsource.com. In order to get the best results when posting or searching for a job, be sure to be as specific as possible. Categories include:

Admin & Customer Support Management, Finance, & Legal Sales & Marketing
Software & IT Web, Design, & Media Writing & Translation

Subcategories are specific fields within an industry. In order to get the best results when posting or searching for a job, be sure to be as specific as possible.

« I can't find the skills I need for my profile. What do I do?

There is a complete list of skills located in the Skills section of your profile. If you are unable to find specific skills within our list, please contact us at support@outsource.com so we can update our list.

« How do I update my profile?

Your profile on Outsource.com will act as a resume that clients will use in order to evaluate your skills and experience. Having a well-developed and strong profile is your key to success. We recommend adding as many relevant skills as possible, constantly updating your portfolio, as well as a well-written description section so clients can get a worthy idea of who you are. Updating your profile is very easy, just follow these simple steps:

  • Log into your Outsource account
  • You will land on your Dashboard page. Click Edit Profile on the left-hand side of the page.
  • From here you can update your description, portfolio, skills, experience, and any other relevant information.

Skills & credentials

« Why are reviews important?

Adding positive reviews to your profile is important because it shows the client that you are credible and reliable. Building trust with your client is a key part of the hiring process and positive reviews will give you a better chance of being hired.

« What can clients write in the reviews?

This is a section where clients can give real reviews of your service. Therefore we encourage the client to be honest, considerate, and give a fair evaluation of your services. If you feel like a client left an unfair review, please contact us at support@outsource.com.

« How do I get reviews?

There are two ways in which you can receive reviews:

1) Request reviews from previous clients
  • Outsource.com allows you to request reviews from previous clients that were outside of Outsource.com in order to help boost your reputation.
2) Reviews from clients on Outsource.com
  • Once a job has been completed, your client is encouraged to leave a review for you about your services. Also, you have the ability to ask the client to leave a review.
« What happens if I get a bad review?

Negative reviews can be considered constructive feedback about how a client feels about your services. The best way to turn a negative review into a positive experience is to strengthen the relationship between you and the client while demonstrating your commitment to satisfaction. If you feel as if a review is not valid or fair, please contact us at support@outsource.com.

« How do I improve my credibility on Outsource.com?

As a freelancer on Outsource.com, the best way to improve your credibility is to add client reviews to your profile. By adding reviews, clients will be able to see what real people had to say about their experiences with you. Often times this can be more powerful than adding excessive skills and experience.
